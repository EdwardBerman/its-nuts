General Thoughts
I'm tired of people over thinking intro tracks. This is just a good ass intro!

Specifics
Cocoa Butter Kisses is the best track on this record (forgiving me for sounding like Anthony Fantano for calling it a record I've always wanted to do that), but I adore this song. It's a very fitting introduction to the rest of the album, upbeat piano strokes, it says "we're back" and "even better than we were the last time", which, are two things I think anyone wants to hear in the introduction to an album. Moreover, Chance holds up his end of the bargain. He is in fact back and better than he was the last time. People often criticize eminem for rapping fast because he has the skillset to do so, even when rapping fast isn't suitable for the song. This song is a great example of how you can rap really fast to generate some momentum, but also slow down to annunciate crucial aspects like with the verses in the middle of the song.
