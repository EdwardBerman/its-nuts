Specifics
I really like this bands style. They claim the genre is post emo, IDK what that means... but the song is cool. But yeah, the song. The screachy tones combined with the upbeat guitar wierdly work. Lyrics are actually pretty thought out. The song weaves in personal monologues with cries out to the person (people) from high school in really clever ways. Moreso the song is being spoken TOO someone, but there are definately parts where the singer is just lamenting to themself. And that's OK, adds some color to the lyrics beyond just some impassioned rant that someone else is supposed to hear.
